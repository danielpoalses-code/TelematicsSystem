The full case

Five reasons fleets switch to Khulu

Most telematics stops at the dot on the map. We start where the wiring loom starts — and we keep going until the whole fleet lives in one place.

We read trucks others can't

Euro 2 workhorses have no CAN bus to plug into — so most providers shrug. Our snap-on ECU reads the truck's native engine sensors directly, no factory electronics required.

We protect to OEM spec

Manufacturer thresholds for temperature, pressure and load — wired into the platform. Drivers get dash warnings before damage happens, not a report after it does.

One login for everything

Cameras, engine data, extra sensors and live tracking in one platform with one UI. No tab-juggling between three vendors to answer one question about one truck.

Your warranty stays intact

The ECU snaps on like a smartwatch — a non-invasive install that doesn't cut, splice or reflash anything the manufacturer cares about. Remove it and the truck is exactly as it was.

Built & answered in South Africa

Engineered for SA routes, SA loads and SA conditions — with local support that picks up the phone. When something breaks at 02:00 on the N3, you talk to people, not a portal.
