The Snap-On ECU

A smartwatch for your truck.

Our snap-on ECU fits trucks that were never built to be connected. It clips on, reads every native sensor the OEM put in the engine, and watches them against factory thresholds — all day, every day.

Reads all native sensors

Coolant, oil pressure, turbo boost, fuel rate, air tanks, gearbox temp — straight from the engine, no guesswork.

Engine protection to OEM spec

Thresholds set to the manufacturer's own limits. Breach one and the driver is warned on the dashboard, instantly.

Snaps on in hours, not days

No rewiring the truck, no downtime drama. Fit it, calibrate it, and the truck is online.

Built for Euro 2 workhorses

The trucks doing the hardest work are usually the blindest. Not anymore — your oldest unit reports like your newest.
