How protection works

Three stages. One rule: the engine never gets a surprise.

Every truck runs the same loop, every second it's alive — baselined to the OEM's own service data, not generic thresholds.

1 · Monitor

Every reading, against OEM spec

Every parameter is sampled continuously by the snap-on ECU and baselined to the manufacturer's own limits for that exact engine — coolant, oil, boost, gearbox, air, charging and more.

2 · Warn

The dash lights up first

The second a limit is crossed, the driver is warned on the dashboard and your ops team gets the same alert on the platform — same data, same moment, no phone call required.

3 · Protect

Escalate before damage

If readings keep climbing, alarms escalate and the driver gets derate guidance — ease off, gear down, pull over — before a hot climb becomes a catastrophic failure.

We never cut the engine. Protection means the right people know in time — the driver stays in control of the truck. Always.
