About De Wet Bikes

Bikes are what we do — all of them

From our branch on Steve Biko Road in Wonderboom South, De Wet Bikes helps riders buy, sell and auction motorcycles, quads, scooters, off-road bikes, go-karts and trailers — plus the odd car and vehicle too.

We stock trusted brands like KTM, Husqvarna, Honda and Kawasaki, back every deal with honest advice, and make finance and trade-ins simple. It's why our customers keep coming back — and why we're 100% recommended.
