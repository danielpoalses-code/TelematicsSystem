About De Wet Bikes

Pretoria's trusted name in two wheels

From our branch on Steve Biko Road in Wonderboom South, De Wet Bikes has spent years helping riders buy, sell and auction motorcycles, quads, scooters, off-road bikes, go-karts and trailers — plus the odd car and vehicle too.

We stock trusted brands like KTM, Husqvarna, Honda and Kawasaki, back every deal with honest advice, and make finance and trade-ins simple. It's why our customers keep coming back — and why we're 100% recommended.
