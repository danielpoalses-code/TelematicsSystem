Good to know

Selling FAQs

What do I need to bring?

Your ID, the bike's registration papers and both sets of keys if you have them. If there's still finance on it, bring your latest settlement letter and we'll help sort it out.

Do you buy bikes that aren't running?

Often, yes. Send us photos and a description on WhatsApp and we'll let you know what we can offer.

Can I trade in instead of selling?

Absolutely. We'll value your current bike and put it straight towards anything in our stock.

How fast do I get paid?

Once you accept the offer and the paperwork is done, we pay out the same day by cash or instant EFT.
